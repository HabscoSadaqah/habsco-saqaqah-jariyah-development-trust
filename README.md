# HABSCO SADAQAH JARIYAH DEVELOPMENT TRUST

## About

HABSCO SADAQAH JARIYAH DEVELOPMENT TRUST is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting sustainable charitable initiatives, community development, education, humanitarian assistance, and other social welfare activities for the benefit of society.

## Mission

To create lasting positive impact through charitable projects, community empowerment, education support, humanitarian relief, and sustainable development initiatives.

## Vision

To become a trusted and impactful organization that transforms lives and strengthens communities through continuous acts of charity and development.

## Objectives

* Support educational development and learning opportunities.
* Provide humanitarian assistance to vulnerable individuals and communities.
* Promote community development projects.
* Encourage sustainable charitable initiatives.
* Foster social welfare and community empowerment.
* Partner with individuals, organizations, and institutions to maximize impact.

## Repository Purpose

This repository contains the source code, documentation, and resources related to the digital platforms and projects of HABSCO SADAQAH JARIYAH DEVELOPMENT TRUST.

## Features

* NGO Website
* Community Outreach Information
* Donation and Support Information
* Project Showcase
* Volunteer Engagement
* Contact and Communication Resources
